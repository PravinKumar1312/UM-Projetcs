Recipe: Biriyani

Required Ingredients:

For marinating the Chicken:

  • 500g chicken (bone-in or boneless)
  • ½ cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Juice of ½ lemon

For Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice (soaked for 30 mins)
  • 4 cups water
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 bay leaf
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste

Other:

  • 2 large onions (thinly sliced)
  • 2-3 tbsp oil or ghee
  • A few strands of saffron soaked in 2 tbsp warm milk (optional)
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp chopped mint leaves

Instruction:

  • Marinate Chicken: Combine all marinade ingredients. Let it rest for at least 30 mins (1-2 hours for best flavor).
  • Cook Rice: Boil water with spices and salt. Add soaked rice and cook till 90% done. Drain and set aside.
  • Fry Onions: In a deep pot, heat oil/ghee. Fry onions until golden brown and crisp. Remove half for layering.
  • Cook Chicken: In the same pot, add marinated chicken and cook for 10-15 mins on medium flame until almost done.
  • Layering: On top of the chicken, add a layer of rice. Sprinkle fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron milk. Cover tightly with a lid (use dough to seal if needed).
  • Dum (Steaming): Cook on a very low flame for 15-20 mins. Let it rest for 10 mins before serving.
